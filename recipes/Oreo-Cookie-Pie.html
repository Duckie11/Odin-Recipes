OREO COOKIE PIE CRUST

INGREDIENTS

  • 30 chocolate creme-filled sandwich cookies, such as OREOÂ®
  • 5 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Add cookies to a food processor and grind into very fine crumbs.
  3. Pour crumbs into a bowl, then pour in melted butter. Mix with a fork until thoroughly combined.
  4. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  5. If making a cheesecake, there's no need to bake the crust. If pre-baking the crust for another use, such as a cream pie, preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (170 degrees C), and bake in the preheated oven until firm, about 15 minutes.
